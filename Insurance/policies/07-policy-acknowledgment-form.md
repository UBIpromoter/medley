## Background Check Consent

Medley Music Access & Inclusion works with minors and individuals with disabilities. As a condition of my engagement with Medley, I authorize The Green Wall Group LLC to conduct the following background checks:

- Massachusetts CORI (Criminal Offender Record Information) check
- Massachusetts SORI (Sex Offender Registry Information) check
- National criminal background check
- National Sex Offender Public Website (NSOPW) search
- Fingerprint-based national criminal history check (FBI/CHSB), if required for host school access

I understand that:

- A criminal record does not automatically disqualify me; results will be reviewed in accordance with Medley's Background Check Policy and Massachusetts law
- If an adverse decision is made based on results, I will be notified in writing and given an opportunity to dispute the accuracy
- Background check results are confidential and will be stored securely
- Checks will be renewed every 3 years during my association with Medley
